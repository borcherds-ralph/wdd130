Overview

Purpose

The website will convey the fun that someone may experience on the river. We will give the times of year we run, sell the supplies people need, and promote river running as a whole. This is an fun activity for the entire family to enjoy. It is a safe activity for all no matter if they are fit or handicapped. One day and two day trips will be the most advertised as well as week long trips will be advertised for the more adventurous individuals. The main message of our site will be: “Rafting is a fun activity for all to enjoy. Come and experience it for yourself and bring your family & friends.”

  • To maintain sustainable growth at all levels of our operations
  • To operate responsible tourism
  • To give the highest quality of service possible to our clientele
  • To involve our local community in our programs
  • To conserve and protect the environment and all that lives within
  • To provide ongoing training for our staff and guides

Audience

Target audience:

  • Adventurous
  • Mid 20's to 50
  • Upper middle class to upper class income
  • Healthy lifestyle
  • Family oriented for one and two day trips
  • Single professionals for week long trips
  • These people want a fun adventure to share with family or friends/co-workers
  • They want to experience something new to them
  • They want to re-experience the adrenaline rush you get while rafting
  • Buy outdoor supplies relating to water activities

    • The site has to be desktop and mobile friendly. If it does not work on cell phones and tablets, Google will not give it high rankings in the SEarch Engine Results Page (SERP).
