Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to show others waht teh youth of our stake did to learn about the pioneers that crossed the plains during the years 1847 to 1851. This was a re-enactment of them pulling handcarts and along the way learn about some of the stories and things that they went through.

Audience

Any person that is interested in learning about this activity and in teaching the youth about what the early members of teh church endured while crossing the plains to get to Salt Lake Valley.
