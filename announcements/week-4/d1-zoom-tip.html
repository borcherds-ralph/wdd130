Here is a tip for this weeks work.

You will be setting a maximum width for the content in week 4. Most students do not have a monitor larger than 17 in. The width is 1600px which is the standard width for at 17 in monitor.

The tip is this.

On Windows and Linux in any web browser: CTRL and -  (control key and the minus key)

MAC :  Press + Ctrl + f.

This will zoom out on the web page you are looking at.
