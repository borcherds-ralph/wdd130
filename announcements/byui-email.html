How to access BYU-Idaho e-mail

as a PathwayConnect student

Login into the Pathway Worldwide portal

Look at the top menu bar in the center and change it to say BYU-Idaho. The default is PathwayConnect

Then click on your name in the top right corner and then select Go To Email

This will log you into your BYU-Idaho email which is on the Microsoft 365 Servers. This is an outlook e-mail. You can forward all these e-mails to your personal e-mail.

BYU-Idaho uses this e-mail for communications to you from the school. You will get at least 2 e-mails every week into this e-mail account. YOu must get into the habit of checking it at least twice a week.

For those of you the do not like email. This is the most common form of communication and you must get used to look at it as you will be using this in business. I have 9 email accounts that I have to check weekly. I have that many as I have a few clients that require me to have an email account on their system for security reasons.
